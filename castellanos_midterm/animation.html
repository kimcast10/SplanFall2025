What do you know about Calico Cats?

Physical Attributes:


Weight: Up to 12 lbs
Length: Up to 18 in
Life Span: About 15 years
Origin: Possibly Egypt and Mediterranean

Characteristics:

  • Highly affectionate
  • Highly intelligent
  • Highly playful
  • Medium energy levels
  • Medium shedding
  • Medium vocalization

Calico Cats Throughout The World

Because of its colors, the Calico cat has become the official state cat of Maryland as of 2001 by the Maryland House of Delegates.

In Japan, a calico cat named Tama became the first feline stationmaster equipped with her very own stationmasters hat!

In both the US and UK, most believe calico cats to be symbols of fortune and luck, Ireland even believes they have wart healing properties after being rubbed.
