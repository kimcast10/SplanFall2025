Bienvenue à Le Petite Croissant

Our Story

Opening its doors in 2025, Le Petite Croissant is a charming French café and bistro nestled in the heart of New York City. Inspired by the cafés of Paris, we bring together classic French flavors, fresh ingredients, and a relaxed atmosphere. Our space is designed to invite guests to slow down, savor, and enjoy the simple pleasures of French cuisine.

Our Menu

Our menu features traditional French café fare including buttery croissants and seasonal entrées. We proudly serve expertly brewed coffees, artisanal cocktails, and a curated selection of French wines and cheeses.

From breakfast to dinner, each dish is made with authentic ingredients and French culinary techniques, every plate reflects the art of French cooking.

Café Experience

Our interior is inspired by the quaint cafés of Paris, with rustic wood tables, elegant chairs, and charming décor that transports you to the streets of France. During warmer months, outdoor seating is available for guests who wish to enjoy the city ambiance while sipping their favorite coffee or wine.

Chef’s Notes

All pastries are baked fresh each morning using traditional French techniques. Our chef recommends pairing a warm croissant with a café au lait or enjoying our seasonal escargots with a classic French 75.

Seasonal ingredients inspire our rotating specials, and every dish is prepared with attention to detail. Guests are encouraged to try new menu items each visit, discovering the depth and variety of French cuisine.
